Craftwood Text is simply dummy text used in typography and layout design. This text has been a standard filler for centuries, ever since an unknown typesetter jumbled up characters to create a sample page. It has endured through the ages, seamlessly transitioning into digital publishing while remaining largely unchanged. Its popularity surged in the mid-20th century with the introduction of specialized font sets, and later with the rise of digital design and desktop publishing software.
